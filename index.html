How It Works

NovaSTEM delivers interactive, age-appropriate experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology—designed to make abstract concepts tangible.

About the Founder

NovaSTEM was founded to make science engaging, accessible, and memorable for young students. Through hands-on experiments and thoughtful instruction, the goal is to build confidence and curiosity early—before students decide STEM “isn’t for them.”
