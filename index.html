How It Works

NovaSTEM delivers interactive, age-appropriate experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology—designed to make abstract concepts tangible through hands-on exploration.

Our Impact

Through hands-on learning, NovaSTEM helps students build confidence, curiosity, and a lasting interest in science.

About the Founder

NovaSTEM was founded with a simple goal: to make science engaging, accessible, and genuinely exciting for young students.

After seeing how early exposure to hands-on experiments could change the way students perceive STEM, NovaSTEM was created to bring that experience directly into classrooms and community spaces.

The mission is not just to teach content, but to inspire confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong interest in learning.
